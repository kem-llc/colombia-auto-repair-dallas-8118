Tire Rotation & Alignment

Extend tire life and improve handling.

Schedule this service

Overview

Proper tire maintenance ensures even wear, better fuel economy, and safer handling. We rotate your tires according to manufacturer recommendations and perform precision alignments to keep your vehicle tracking straight.

What's included

  • Even tire wear and longer tread life
  • Improved gas mileage
  • Smoother ride and better handling
  • Reduced strain on suspension components

How it works

1

Inspection

We inspect tires for wear patterns, pressure, and damage.

2

Rotation

We rotate tires to the recommended positions to promote even wear.

3

Alignment

Using laser alignment equipment, we adjust angles to factory specifications.

Common questions

How often should I rotate my tires?

Every 5,000 to 7,500 miles, or at each oil change.

Will alignment fix a pulling steering wheel?

Yes, misalignment often causes pulling. We'll adjust it so your car drives straight.
