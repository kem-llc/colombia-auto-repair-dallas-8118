Our story

Your Trusted Local Auto Mechanic in Dallas

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe and mobile.

Colombia Auto Repair has been serving the Dallas community for over 15 years. Founded by a team of passionate mechanics, our shop was built on the principles of integrity, transparency, and technical excellence. We started as a small garage and grew through word-of-mouth because our customers trust us to treat them fairly.

Today, we continue to invest in the latest tools and training to service all makes and models. Whether it's a routine oil change or a complex engine repair, we approach every job with the same dedication. Our loyal customers return because they know we stand behind our work and genuinely care about their safety.
